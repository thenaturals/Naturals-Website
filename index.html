Naturally SINGING
A London-based A Cappella group with a passion for Choral, Jazz and Modern music

ABOUT US




We are a group of young professionals who have found a creative outlet in A Cappella. Having met through the years at school or university, we now perform together at regular functions. From 1700 to 2017, we sing a variety of musical genres, all without accompaniment!

We are available for functions and events, and can create bespoke arrangements of your favorite songs, or you can pick from our set list.

(Photo) With Rt Hon David Mundell MP, at his annual Christmas event, where we sang a selection of modern and traditional carols.

SET LIST
